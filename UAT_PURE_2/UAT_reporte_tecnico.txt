CÁLCULOS FUNDAMENTALES:

1. ESCALAS PLANCK:
   • Longitud Planck: 1.616e-35 m
   • Tiempo Planck: 5.391e-44 s
   • Área Planck: 2.612e-70 m²
   • Entropía Planck: 3.452e-24 J/K

2. DERIVACIÓN DE CONSTANTES:
   • C_S_UAT = (S_planck / t_planck) × κ_crit
             = (3.452e-24 / 5.391e-44) × 1.00e-78
             = 6.402e-59 J/(K s)

   • C_CPU = C_UAT / C_S_UAT
           = 1.081000e-03 / 6.402e-59
           = 1.688e+55 s/J

   • k_early = 1 + C_CPU × C_S_UAT × log₁₀(1/κ_crit)
             = 1 + (1.688e+55) × (6.402e-59) × 78.0
             = 1.084318

3. EQUILIBRIO TERMODINÁMICO:
   • Ṡ_standard = S_planck / t_planck = 6.402e+19 J/(K s)
   • Ṡ_causal = C_S_UAT × (1/κ_crit) = 6.402e+19 J/(K s)
   • Ṡ_net = Ṡ_standard - Ṡ_causal = 0.0e+00 J/(K s)
